Esri’s ArcGIS™ platform
provides a range of capabilities.

The technical solution is based on the spatial data infrastructure technology provided by Esri’s ArcGIS platform. The ArcGIS platform is an integrated web-GIS system that provides a wide range of capabilities for storing, managing, analysing, visualising and sharing geo-spatial data for all users within the organisation as well as public users.
The platform provides the framework of data and technology needed to address a wide range of location related business processes.

Esri’s ArcGIS™ platform

An integrated Web-GIS technology.

Provides a range of capabilities.

The technical solution is based on the spatial data infrastructure technology provided by Esri’s ArcGIS™ platform. The ArcGIS™ platform is an integrated Web-GIS system that provides a wide range of capabilities for storing, managing, analysing, visualising and sharing geo-spatial data for all users within the organisation as well as public users.
The platform provides the framework of data and technology needed to address a wide range of location related business processes.

A hosting framework for data.

The ArcGIS™ platform is designed to be a hosting framework for data and applications. The methodology includes all necessary components to configure this framework including the loading and dissemination of geo-spatial datasets. It does also include the building, configuration and deployment of focussed apps.

Esri South Africa &
Agizo Solutions JV.

In response to this bid, a joint venture was formed to leverage the GIS technology competencies of Esri South Africa and the integrated development, spatial planning and modelling competencies of Agizo Solutions.

Esri South Africa have established themselves as the leader in GIS technology over the past 20 years. Esri has the largest pool of GIS expertise in Southern Africa and their technical staff continue to stay abreast of the latest GIS technology developments and applications used by top companies throughout the world.
Agizo Solutions provides professional consultancy in the areas of strategic spatial planning, land use planning and management, development planning, geographic information systems, land surveying and project management.

Author Services

Web services are a method of sharing geospatial content across the web.

Host Services

Services provide a ‘live view’ into the geodatabase in a very fast and reliable way.

Host Items

The portal is the place where users can go to discover, use, make and share maps.

Create Web Maps

A web map is a user-created combination of items from the portal that creates a “mash-up” of geospatial information.

Create Applications

Platform apps are simply web maps that are presented to the user for a particular, focussed purpose.
