Want to get more involved...?  then become an active participant.

Not only do we welcome your participation, but we actively encourage and support those who want to do so. Through the lessons learnt and shared from your own experience, you’ll potentially be helping us influence and improve upon the experience’s of the entire community of ecosystem users.

First and foremost, please submit meaningful and relevant suggestions, with emphasis on the context and content (i.e., interoperability, usability enhancements, technical improvements, or even unidentified bugs and errors).

Based on your feedback’s subject-matter, a member of our project team will be in touch as soon as possible!
